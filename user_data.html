Frequently Asked Questions

What information is available when I request my data?

In this file, you will find your basic account information, search history, selfie history, support communications, and other information. Contact us if you have any questions.

What if I think some information is missing?

We try to make sure your newest information is provided, but sometimes there's a delay before we can include it. If you think some information is missing, try downloading your data again in a day or two. If you still don't see it, contact us!

What other information can I access?

In addition to the information available here, Bitmoji also retains logs of activities on our services, like when you click on a trait in the avatar builder or save an outfit. We use these logs to understand how our services are being used so we can provide and improve our apps, report aggregate metrics, and improve the security of our services.

Typically, logs are retained for 180 days but, in some cases, we may need to retain these logs for much longer. For example, we may need to store some logs for the lifetime of your account. We provide counts of the most important activity logs here, like the number of times you've opened Bitmoji and the number of Bitmoji stickers you've sent. The actual logs are stored pseudonymously and we are unable to associate them with individual users without significant burden.

How can I control my information?

We want you to feel in control when it comes to how we collect and process your data. To help, we provide a number of options - like clearing your history or deleting your account - right in our app!

How is my information shared?

When we share information, it's usually because you ask us to or because we need to share your information to provide our services or comply with the law. You can learn more in our Privacy Policy.

I have another privacy question.

We'd love to provide an answer! Head to our Privacy Policy, or reach out to us.
